About Our Studio

Caring for Your Smile Since 2012

Mothupi Dental Studio was founded with a single purpose: to provide exceptional dental care in an environment where every patient feels welcomed, valued, and at ease. Our experienced team combines clinical expertise with a genuine passion for helping people achieve their healthiest, most confident smiles.

Experienced & Qualified Team

All our practitioners are registered with the HPCSA and committed to continuous professional development.

Advanced Technology

We invest in the latest dental technology — digital X-rays, intraoral cameras, and laser dentistry for superior precision.

Patient-Centred Approach

We listen first. Every treatment plan is personalised to your unique needs, preferences, and comfort level.
